PRECIOUS - American Black and White Shorthair

Precious is the sweetest kitten and loves to cuddle. Precious showed up one day very sick. She was only a couple months old and really in bad shape. With the help of a caring vetenarian and lots of TLC she grew to be a healthy and strong kitten. While we tried to find her owner she ended up becoming a most precious part of our family. As she has aged she has slowed down some, but has become an avid cuddler...purr purr purr.
